about me







Jonas Wienberg is an Interaction Design student at Hochschule für Gestaltung Schwäbisch Gmünd, focusing on interdisciplinary projects that combine design innovation, analytical leadership (PMO), and user-centered solutions (UI/UX).

As Chair of the General Students’ Committee (AStA), he advocates for student interests, leads collaborative initiatives, and organizes institutional events.

His academic background includes certifications in Intercultural Conflict Management, Building Climate Resilience through Ecosystem-based Adaptation Planning, Business Compliance, and Data Privacy. Additionally, he is proficient in Business English at the C1 level.
